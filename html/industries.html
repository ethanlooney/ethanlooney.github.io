Industries Served

Construction

We at Crofts Accounting will help you balance your budget for your projects from big to small. We will help you organize and set up your finances. We accommodate every stage of buisness development, from income tax issues to information systems,financial reporting or bonding needs.

Farming and Agriculture

We at Crofts Accounting will help you manage your farms finances so you can focus on your animals and crops. We will help the farmers stay in buisness and connect them with contacts in the agriculture industry. Our knowledge of the entire agribusiness supply chain, from grower to retailer, allows us to identify areas where we can increase efficiency and deliver solutions to help your business thrive.

Healthcare

We at Crofts Accounting will help local hospitals stay in buisness so they can serve their communities better. We can help the hospitals manage thier finances better so they can focus on the patients rather than the finance side. Our consulting and advisory services are designed to bring knowledgeable and innovative solutions and a higher level of business acumen to private medical practices.

Manufacturing

We at Crofts Accounting will help streamline your manufacturing plants and make paying your employees easier. We will focus on your finances so that you can focus on improving your products. We will help manange the finances behind your buisness so that it will be more transparent and long lasting. We offer a unique blend of manufacturing industry knowledge, technical expertise and valuable solutions focused on addressing the production, operations and regulatory demands of our clients.

Nonprofits

We at Crofts Accounting will help you distribute your fund easier to the specified nonprofits and individuals. We will help manage your enployee pays and finances so that you can focus on finding more nonprofits and branching out to others you may not be involved in already. From nonprofit financial advisory services to nonprofit audits, reviews and compilations, our team of experienced professionals help nonprofit and government organizations meet their compliance and financial goals.

Real Estate

We at Crofts Accounting will help individual agents and agencies to better serve their customers. We will strive to manage finances and assets of both buyers and sellers and agents and agencies so that there will be more trasparency between buyer and seller. Our team of certified public accountants, business advisors, personal financial planners and industry experts can guide you through the complexities of developing and operating real estate. We can also provide suggestions to managers and brokers to improve their financial strength and tax outcomes in their businesses.
